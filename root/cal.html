Topics covered (as the syllabus of Mumbai University revised in August 2019)

3. Semiconductors

5. Superconductivity

Focus of the app is not about students solving physics problems on their fingertips but is about verification of result and referential purpose for students. The app consists of: Physics calculator: Through this feature, students can calculate the result of the problem statement. This feature works best in case of direct problem statements. Formula which is used, is displayed too. Solved examples: It consist of proper step wise solution for indirect and tricky problem statement. Formula list is provided for revision and reference.
